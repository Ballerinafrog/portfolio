Why did I choose this career?

I chose this career because I have always been interested in the field of biology especially the field of microbiology and chemistry.

What does someone in this position do?

Studies the chemical components and processes of living systems plants, insects, viruses, microorganisms, and mammals to explain how and why chemical reactions occur.
